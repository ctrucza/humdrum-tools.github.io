====================================
Humdrum Extras
====================================

No discussion of Humdrum is complete 
without Craig Sapp's `Humdrum Extras <http://extra.humdrum.org/>`_.

These include tools for downloading large corpora
directly to your computer, visual keyscapes, Humdrum-tailored
search tools, converters, and many more.
