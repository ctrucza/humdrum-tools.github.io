This is a preliminary on-line version of a book manuscript by the same name. Printed copies of A User’s Guide are available for purchase from the ` **Center for Computer Assisted Research in the Humanities****`_. In addition, copies of the Humdrum Toolkit Reference Manual are also available from the same source.
