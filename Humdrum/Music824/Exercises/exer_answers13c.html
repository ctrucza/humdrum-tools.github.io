Humdrum Exercise #8 - Answers

All of the following commands assume that you are located in the appropriate score directory.

3.

In the first movement of Corelli's Opus 1, No. 6, what is the highest pitch in measure 10?
COMMAND:     yank -n ^= -r 10 op01n06a.krn | census -k
ANSWER:         E5 (ee)
