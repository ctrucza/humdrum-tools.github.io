Humdrum Exercise #8 - Answers

All of the following commands assume that you are located in the appropriate score directory.

4.

In the Dutch folksong "Kornschneiden ohne Text", what is the highest pitch in the 5th phrase?
COMMAND:     grep '!!!OTL:.*Kornschneiden ohne Text' *.krn
                            yank -o { -r 5 neder084.krn | census -k
ANSWER:         D5 (dd)
